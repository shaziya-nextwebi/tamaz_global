Understanding Glutathione & Vitamin C

Melanocytes are the cells in our skin responsible for producing melanin, the pigment that determines our skin color. Higher melanin levels result in darker skin tones, while lower melanin levels result in lighter skin tones. The production of melanin is influenced by genetic factors.

Glutathione is a natural antioxidant and detoxifying agent in our body. It is a protein-based molecule composed of three amino acids: cysteine, glutamate, and glycine. Glutathione inhibits the enzyme tyrosinase, which is responsible for melanin production. By blocking tyrosinase, Glutathione reduces melanin production, leading to lighter skin. Vitamin C, a key component of Vesco Pharma's Gluta C 1000, enhances the effects of Glutathione by stimulating the skin's cells and promoting hydration. This combination not only whitens the skin but also nourishes it, making it healthier and more radiant.
